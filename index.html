INFURIOUS

About Me

Meet Red, a brave bird on a mission to rescue his friends trapped in a chaotic world filled with obstacles. To save them, Red must fly through a series of challenges, collecting bombs along the way. These bombs are crucial for breaking down barriers that keep his friends captive.

But it’s not an easy journey! With each obstacle he passes, Red's speed increases, making it trickier to navigate. Players must help Red avoid crashing into obstacles while gathering as many bombs as possible to ensure a successful rescue. Can you guide Red safely through this risky adventure and save his friends?

How to Play

It’s may seem easy to start playing, but hard to master:

Tap or click to make Red fly upwards.

Avoid obstacles by timing your taps carefully.

Collect bombs to earn bonus points: 2 points for passing a pole and 1 point for collecting a bomb.

The longer you play, the faster the obstacles come at you!
